Anime Genres

People who don't know that much about anime may think anime is just a single small community with limited show and genres but that is where they are wrong.

There are thousands of differnt anime series, countless genres and unique communities out there.

Some of the differnt genres include:

  • Action
  • Comedy
  • Slice-of-Life
  • Drama
  • Psychological
  • Horror
  • History
  • Military
  • Mecha (giant robots)
  • Supernatural/Magic
  • Romance
  • Sci-Fi
  • Martial Arts
  • Isekai (Anoter world)
  • Post-Apocalyptic
  • Music
  • Parody
  • Shounen (For young males)
  • Shoujo (For young females)
  • Seinen (For adult men)
  • Vampires
  • Time travel

And their are many many more.
